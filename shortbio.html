Grace Koziol Cartographer

I am an undergraduate student at the University of Iowa majoring in Environmental Policy and Planning and minoring in Environmental Science and GIS. In addition, I have passions for wildlife conservation and community development, which I hope to incorporate into my future career. As an undergrad, I have been lucky enough to have had opportunities to explore various avenues in my field. I became familiar with zero-waste practices and policies through my fellowship with the Post-Landfill Action Network and have applied GIS to real-world projects through my research with the Iowa Community Integrated Geography Organization. These experiences have led me to intern at Brookfield Zoo Chicago and, most recently, the DuPage County Division of Transportation.
